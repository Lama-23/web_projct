Activities

Swimming and diving

Sandala Island is distinguished by its white sandy beaches and clear turquoise waters, making it an ideal destination for swimming and diving. You can enjoy the beauty of coral reefs and colorful underwater fish.

Surfing

Sundaleh Island is a great destination for surfing, as it is characterized by strong and varied waves. You can try surfing on a wakeboard or kitesurfing board.

Kayaking

You can explore the island by kayaking. You can paddle around the island or go on an excursion to nearby islands.

Yacht Trips

Sendala Neum Island is an ideal destination for yacht trips. You can rent your own yacht or go on a cruise with a tour company.

Hiking

You can enjoy the picturesque nature of the island by hiking. There are several hiking trails on the island, including one that leads to the top of the mountain in the center of the island.

Relaxing on the beach

You can relax on one of the beautiful beaches on the island and enjoy the stunning views.
