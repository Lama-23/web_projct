About the Island

On December 5, 2022, Prince Mohammed bin Salman announced the development of Oxagon Industrial City in NEOM is a catalyst for economic growth and diversification in the Kingdom in general, meeting ambitions to achieve the goals of Vision 2030. Oxagon will contribute to redefining the world's direction towards future industrial development, as well as contributing to environmental protection, job creation and growth. It will also participate in supporting the Kingdom in the field of regional trade, in addition to supporting global trade flows in the region.

Industrial Model

Sectors Destination Advanced and clean

OXAGON will be the gateway to NEOM's advanced and clean sectors and industries, where we will adopt unprecedented methodologies at the highest international levels to enable the factories of the future to manufacture the products of the future. We will build an integrated industrial ecosystem that supports companies and businesses in their manufacturing journey with the latest and most advanced technologies.

Research & Innovation

We build a ecosystem that includes innovators and entrepreneurs and enables them to apply ideas, and transfer them from experimentation in laboratories to markets, in an advanced and collaborative digital society. The R&I ecosystem will provide state-of-the-art facilities that enable innovators on their journey from idea to commercialization through state-of-the-art facilities to support entrepreneurs, project development, manufacturing, and with the help of a network of global experts.

Port & Chains Supply & Services Logistics

OXAGON will build the world's first advanced and sustainable digital port that embraces next-generation technologies, integrates supply chains, logistics and a freight delivery system by train, enhancing connectivity and access to global trade routes. Our supply chain platform will also serve NEOM's population and economy under the umbrella of a unified network both physically and digitally.

Prosperous communities

OXAGON is designing a new model for urban living. By redefining the concept of traditional industrial cities, we have a historic opportunity to start from scratch to develop mixed-use communities centered around people, innovation and sustainability.
